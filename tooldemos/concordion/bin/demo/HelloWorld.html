As a quick example, in order to verify that Concordion works, I want to execute a string concatenation check

When the action is Hello and the subject is World then the greeting should be Hello, World!.

Or, said differently:

The greeting Hello, World! is the right one when the action is Hello and the subject is World.

When the separator is !, the action is Hello and the subject is World then the greeting should be Hello! World!.
